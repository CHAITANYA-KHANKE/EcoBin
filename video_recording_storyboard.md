## ⏱️ Video Timeline & Recording Script

### 🎬 Part 1: Introduction (0:00 - 0:12)
* **What to show on screen**: 
  * Open your browser to `http://localhost:3000/`.
  * Start with the mouse pointer near the top header: **"CommunityPulse AI"**.
  * Hover over the KPI blocks (Collected Tons, Avg Fill Rate, Environmental Impact).
* **What to say**:
  > "Hello everyone! Today, I’m excited to show you CommunityPulse AI: EcoBin Edition. This is an AI-powered Decision Intelligence Platform built for smart city waste operations, localized for Chandrapur, Maharashtra."

### 🎬 Part 2: NVIDIA RAPIDS Optimization (0:12 - 0:32)
* **What to show on screen**:
  * Scroll down on the right panel to locate the **"Route Optimization"** box.
  * Click the green/blue **"Optimize Collection Route"** button.
  * Wait 2 seconds: see the dashed cyan route line draw on the leaflet map.
  * Point your mouse to the **NVIDIA RAPIDS Telemetry Benchmark** box and hover over the speedup factor (e.g. *29x Faster*).
* **What to say**:
  > "To minimize fuel and transit costs, we trigger our Route Optimizer. This solves a Traveling Salesperson problem to calculate the shortest path. Powered by NVIDIA RAPIDS cuDF on the backend, it aggregates millions of IoT pings in milliseconds, achieving a massive twenty-nine times speedup compared to standard CPU processing."

### 🎬 Part 3: AI Forecasting Engine (0:32 - 0:48)
* **What to show on screen**:
  * Scroll up slightly on the right panel.
  * Click the **Smart Bin Dropdown** and select **"BIN-002"** or **"BIN-001"**.
  * Show the interactive cyan Area Chart that renders the predicted accumulation curves.
  * Hover over the chart tooltips to show the time-stamped predictions.
* **What to say**:
  > "Next, look at the AI Forecasting Engine. By selecting any smart bin, our model predicts waste accumulation trends twenty-four hours in advance, allowing municipal crews to proactively schedule cleanups before any overflow occurs."

### 🎬 Part 4: Citizen Portal & OpenAI Vision (0:48 - 1:12)
* **What to show on screen**:
  * Scroll to the tabs menu and click the red **"Citizen Portal"** tab.
  * Click the **"Gandhi Chowk Market"** coordinates selector button.
  * Type in the description box: *"Heavy trash pile and plastic bottles blocking the shop entrance."*
  * Click the red **"File Citizen Incident"** button.
  * Wait 3 seconds: scroll down to show the green **"OpenAI Vision Results"** card with the AI Category (*Recyclable/Organic*) and Technical Breakdown description.
* **What to say**:
  > "We also empower residents. Through the Citizen Portal, users can report waste piles. The uploaded photo is analyzed in real-time by the OpenAI Vision API, which instantly validates the waste, determines the category, rates the hazard level, and maps it for collection."

* **What to show on screen**:
  * Scroll up and click the green **"Decision Chat"** tab.

## 💡 Quick Tips for Recording:
1. **Resolution**: Keep your browser window maximized.
2. **Speed**: Move your mouse cursor slowly and smoothly so the viewer can follow your actions.
3. **Sound**: If you are using an AI Voice generator, upload the text in sections matching the timestamps above, then merge them with your video recording in an editor (like CapCut, Premiere, or Canva).
